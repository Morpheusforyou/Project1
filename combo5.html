Stuff bread

Calories: 400 kcal

Fat: 18g

Protein: 12g

Carbs: 50g

This savory stuffed bread is filled with a delicious pumpkin mix, featuring a combination of mashed pumpkin, spices, and a touch of sweetness. The bread is soft and fluffy, while the stuffing provides a unique burst of flavors. Perfect for a light yet filling meal!

The combination of pumpkin, aromatic spices, and fresh herbs makes this dish a healthy and tasty option for anyone looking for comfort food with a twist.
